About the author:

My name is Collin DeSantis. I live in & grew up in New Jersey.

I graduated high school early at 16 with the goal of teaching myself about the world and myself instead. Since then I have worked across a wide variety of trades and projects. Some examples are game development, server administration, vehicle repair, and parametric CAD. The list goes on and I intend to write about all of these topics on this site's catalogue.

Some more about me: I ride a Suzuki DR650 motorcycle as my main form of transportation. I have been running linux on all of my devices for years. I spend my free time reading and thinking about history and philosophy. I source raw milk from the Amish in PA as my primary source of nutrition.

At the moment I am contributing to Open Source Ecology. On the side I am developing a small ROBLOX game and preparing for a cross-country motorcycle trip.

This site functions as my personal portfolio and blog station. You can read more about it here.

I can be reached at c@collindesantis.com.

This site was created as a place to properly present & log my various projects and ideas. It is running in my garage on a raspberry pi. I use no external services or pay for any subscriptions. It is entirely self hosted.

If you are a particularly shrewd viewer you may have noticed that this site has no load times. I archited it his way on purpose - to be the antithesis of the modern web's ethos. Each page is static and the contained content is woven to speak for itself. I write each article with my own hands. No AI is used for writing purposes.

In the future I plan to upgrade to solar power as a small science project. As it grows I will add a variety of amenities that are currently just a pile of ideas sitting in my head.
